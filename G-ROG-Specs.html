Asus ROG Zephyrus G15

SPECIFICATION

  • Processor: AMD Ryzen 9 5900HS
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce RTX 3070
  • Memory: 16GB RAM
  • Storage: 1TB SSD
  • Display: 15.6" QHD 165Hz

FEATURES

  • Performance: The Ryzen 9 5900HS and RTX 3070 combination provides high performance for gaming and other demanding tasks.
  • Design: Sleek and lightweight for a gaming laptop, making it relatively portable.
  • Battery Life: Better than many gaming laptops, allowing for some use without being plugged in.
  • Cooling: Efficient cooling system to maintain performance during intense gaming sessions.
  • Audio: Quality speakers with Dolby Atmos support for an immersive audio experience.
